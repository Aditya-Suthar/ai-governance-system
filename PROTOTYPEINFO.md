# 🚀 Project Zero Point
AI-Powered Governance & Citizen Complaint Management System

---

## 📌 Problem
Government complaint systems are slow, unorganized, and lack prioritization.  
Citizens submit issues but authorities struggle to analyze and respond efficiently.

---

## 💡 Solution
Project Zero Point is an **AI-driven complaint management platform** that allows citizens to report issues with location data and enables authorities to analyze complaints using intelligent dashboards.

---

## ⚙️ Features

### 👤 Citizen Side
- Submit complaints
- Upload images
- Automatic location tagging
- Complaint status tracking

### 🏛 Authority Dashboard
- View all complaints
- Update complaint status
- Priority management
- Category assignment
- Complaint heatmap visualization

### 🤖 AI Features

✅ Implemented AI Features

AI Complaint Categorization
Automatically detects complaint category such as Road, Electricity, Water, Sanitation, etc.

AI Priority Assignment
AI analyzes complaint text and assigns priority levels (Low, Medium, High).

Hotspot Detection
Identifies areas with the highest number of complaints using location-based analysis.

AI Complaint Insights Bot
Chatbot that allows authorities to query complaint data using natural language.

Smart Keyword Extraction
AI extracts important keywords from complaint descriptions for better analysis.

Complaint Trend Analysis
AI analyzes complaint trends over time to identify common civic issues.

Location-based Issue Detection
AI groups complaints from the same area to highlight problematic locations.

Automated Complaint Tagging
AI automatically tags complaints with relevant labels like urgent, infrastructure, or public safety.

Complaint Data Analytics
AI generates insights on complaint distribution by category, district, and status.

AI-based Complaint Search Assistance
Allows authorities to search complaints intelligently using AI-assisted query understanding.



UPCOMING AI FEATURES🤖:-

Smart Complaint Summarization
AI automatically converts long citizen complaints into short, clear summaries for faster review by authorities.

Duplicate Complaint Detection
AI detects multiple complaints about the same issue (e.g., same pothole or broken light) and groups them together to reduce spam and improve issue tracking.

Emergency Complaint Detection
AI identifies urgent issues such as fires, accidents, flooding, or electrical hazards and automatically escalates them for immediate action.

AI Department Recommendation
AI analyzes complaint text and automatically assigns the most relevant government department (e.g., Electricity, Sanitation, Water, Roads).

Complaint Severity Prediction
AI predicts how severe a complaint is and assigns priority levels like Low, Medium, High, or Critical.

AI Heatmap Prediction
Using historical complaint data, AI predicts future complaint hotspots so authorities can take preventive actions.

Citizen Trust Score System
AI evaluates user credibility based on complaint history to detect spam or fake reports.

AI Image Analysis for Complaints
AI analyzes uploaded complaint images to detect issues like potholes, garbage piles, broken street lights, etc.

Smart Complaint Routing
AI automatically forwards complaints to the correct authority or department based on category, location, and urgency.

AI Governance Assistant (Chatbot for Authorities)
Authorities can ask questions about complaint data in natural language and receive instant insights
